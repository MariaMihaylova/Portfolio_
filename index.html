About Me

My name is Maria Mihaylova, and I am currently doing a bachelor in ICT at Fontys University of Applied Sciences. I originally come from Bulgaria, like probably over 60% of Fontys students. I have been in the Netherlands for almost 3 years. I started my higher education in Software Engineering and this semester I have decided to switch to Media Design. I have previously worked with different languages such as C#, Python, SQL, R, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and other. In this portfolio you will be able to find some of my work since I have started my Media Design journey. Enjoy!
