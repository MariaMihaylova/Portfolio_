Media production

Personal reflection

This semester's Media Design course has been an enriching and fulfilling experience for me. Throughout the course, I have delved into various aspects of media production, including logo design, photography, and videography.

The individual logo design assignment was a great opportunity to showcase my creativity and design skills. Creating a logo for a brand of my choice allowed me to explore different visual elements and concepts. This project challenged me to think critically about brand identity and create a visually appealing logo that communicates the brand's message effectively.

In the realm of photography, I had the opportunity to create a photography portfolio photo book. This project involved using a DSLR camera and performing various tasks to capture compelling images. Through this assignment, I learned about composition, lighting, and storytelling within the medium of photography. The photo book served as a culmination of my photography skills, presenting a collection of visually stunning images that showcased my ability to capture captivating moments.

The videography assignments were equally engaging and allowed me to explore the art of storytelling through moving images. One of the notable projects was the creation of a mini documentary on the housing situation in the Netherlands. Collaborating with my group, we brainstormed ideas, created a storyboard, and filmed scenes to bring our vision to life. This project enabled me to develop skills in filming, editing, and understanding the power of visual storytelling.

Overall, this Media Design course has provided a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of media production. From logo design to photography and videography, I have developed skills in visual communication, storytelling, and user-centered design. The hands-on projects and assignments have challenged me to think creatively, develop technical expertise, and understand the impact of media design on audience perception.
